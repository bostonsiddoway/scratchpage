Appetizer

Boston's Famous Onion Rings

  • 1 tsp baking Powder
  • 1 Tsp Salt
  • 1 C Milk
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 3/4 C Panko
  • 1/4 tsp Pepper
  • 1/4 tsp Season Salt
  1. Slice Onion into thin rings
  2. To create dry powder, mix:
    • Flour
    • Salt
    • Baking Powder
  3. Mix eggs and milk to create wet mix
  4. Preheat oil to 365
  5. Dip each onion ring into dry powder
  6. Mix leftover dry powder into wet mix
  7. Dip each onion into wet mix
  8. Dip each ring into panko mix and seasonings
  9. Fry onion rings on each side for 1-2 mins
  10. Enjoy!




Entree

Dad's Lomo Saltado

  • 2 TB Lomo Saltado
  • 1 Lb Flank Steak
  • 1 Onion Sliced
  • 1 Medium Bell Pepper
  • 1 Small Tomato
  • 2 cloves of minced garlic
  • 1 small, minced serrano pepper
  • 2 TB Soy Sauce
  • 1 TB Vinegar
  • 2 TB Chopped Cilantro
  • 1 LB Crinkle Cut Fries
  • 4 Cups of Cooked white rice
  1. Cook French Fries in Air Fry as package instructs
  2. Begin cooking 1 cup of white rice per person
  3. Add Oil to pan and brown steak with salt and pepper
  4. Remove steak and add sliced peppers and onion to pan
  5. Once softened, add tomato, garlic, serrano pepper and cook for 5 minutes
  6. Add Soy suace and vinegar, cook for another minute
  7. Add steak and Cilantro to pan
  8. Pour steak, vegatables, and juices over frys and rice.
  9. Mix and enjoy!

Dessert

Mom's Simple Pumpkin Cookies

  • 1 Large Can of Pumpkin Filling
  • 1 Bag of White Chocolate Chips
  • 1 Box of Spice Cake Mix
  1. Pre-heat Oven to 350
  2. Mix spice mix and chocolate chips together
  3. Mix in Pumpkin Filling
  4. Scoop into 24 cookies
  5. Bake for 10-12 Minutes
